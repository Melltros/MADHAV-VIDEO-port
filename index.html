How I Work

MY
PROCESS

Every project I take on follows a clear creative system — from the first idea to the final rendered frame or deployed site.

01

Brief & Discovery

I start by deeply understanding your vision, goals, and target audience. I ask the right questions so every creative decision has a purpose.

02

Storyboard & Plan

Before any tool is opened, I map out the structure — whether it's a rough cut sequence or a site wireframe. Planning saves time and creates better results.

03

Create & Craft

This is where the magic happens. I dive into Premiere Pro, After Effects, or VS Code and build the thing — with obsessive attention to every detail.

04

Refine & Polish

Great work is 80% polish. I review, tweak, and iterate until every transition, color, or interaction feels exactly right.

05

Deliver & Launch

Final delivery in your preferred format — optimized video exports, or a fully deployed, fast-loading web project ready to impress.
